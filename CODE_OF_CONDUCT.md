# Code of Conduct

## Standard

Be respectful, direct, and professional. Keep discussion focused on building reliable defensive software and documentation.

## Not Acceptable

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Requests to weaponize the project
- Sharing secrets, private credentials, or sensitive deployment details
- Encouraging illegal radio operation or unsafe hardware use

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block contributors who violate this standard.
